About Jusgesto

Jusgesto is an AI-powered legal assistance platform designed to help individuals understand their legal problems, discover similar past cases, and connect with the most suitable lawyers.

The system uses Machine Learning techniques such as TF-IDF vectorization and Nearest Neighbors to analyze user queries, retrieve relevant legal precedents, and recommend lawyers based on domain-specific expertise.

Our goal is to make legal guidance more accessible, transparent, and data-driven.

Technology Stack

  • FastAPI (Backend)
  • Machine Learning (TF-IDF + KNN)
  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Frontend)
  • Python & Scikit-Learn
